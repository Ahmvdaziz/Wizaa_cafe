Our Story

More Than Coffee.
A Philosophy.

We believe a perfect cup of coffee is a meditation — a ritual that deserves reverence, time, and artistry.

Founded in 2015 in the heart of Paris's Golden Triangle, Velour Café was born from a single obsession: to create moments that linger long after the last sip. Our master baristas train for years under world-class mentors including former World Barista Champions, our beans are sourced from the world's finest micro-farms through direct trade relationships, and our interiors were designed by Atelier Bardot — Paris's celebrated studio — to make you forget the rush of the city outside.

Every detail — from the hand-thrown ceramic cups sourced from a Provençal atelier to the bespoke jazz and classical playlist curated by our resident musician that evolves with the hour — is intentional. At Velour, coffee is not just a beverage. It is a ceremony.

The Velour Way

An Experience Beyond the Cup

Every detail at Velour is intentional — from the music to the temperature of the light. Nothing is left to chance.

Ethically Sourced

Every bean is traceable to a single farm and a named farmer. We pay 40% above fair-trade market prices and visit our partner farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala every year.

In-House Roasting

Our roastery is visible from the café floor. Beans are roasted in 10kg small batches twice weekly on a vintage Probat drum roaster, ensuring unmatched freshness in every cup.

Award-Winning Baristas

Our team has competed and placed top-three in the World Barista Championship three consecutive years. Your cup is in exceptionally skilled hands.

Curated Atmosphere

Bespoke playlists shift from soft jazz in the morning to ambient electronica by evening. Lighting is tuned by the hour. Acoustics are designed for calm, intimate conversation.

Barista Workshops

Join our monthly masterclasses — open to all levels. Learn the science of extraction, the art of latte pours, and the ritual of the perfect pour-over, guided by our champions.

Private Events

Our private salon seats up to 24 guests and can be reserved for coffee tastings, intimate celebrations, corporate mornings, or bespoke dining experiences curated by our chef.
